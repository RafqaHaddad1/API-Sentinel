How the Model Works
  • Step 1 The proxy intercepts each API request before it reaches the backend service.
  • Step 2 The system extracts request features such as method, endpoint, headers, payload, token behavior, response time, and anomaly scores.
  • Step 3 The supervised model detects known attack patterns, while the unsupervised model identifies unusual behavior.
  • Risk Decision Scores are combined into a final risk level: Low, Medium, or High Risk.
  • Action Low risk requests are allowed, medium risk requests are monitored, and high risk requests can be blocked or escalated.
